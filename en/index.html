Business Law

We assist businesses of all sizes, from the family business to large multinationals, along with small-to-medium enterprises in their most complex legal issues.

With our rich experience representing clients in a variety of industries and markets, our attorneys act as partners in the life of your business, from incorporation to mergers/acquisitions and dissolution, along with day-to-day operations.

By integrating our keen knowledge of the business world, marked with thorough understanding of our clients’ businesses and their strategic requirements, we ensure that they receive a seamless service and high quality performances that meet today’s often challenging business needs.

General Litigation

Our attorneys have extensive litigation experience in representing the interests of their clients in various civil cases , including civil liability (tort), contractual and non-performance disputes , as well as a wide variety of contentious matters.

Karavoulias Attorneys regularly appear before all judicial instances ( Court of Québec, Superior Court, Court of Appeal ) and administrative tribunals (whether the Régie des alcools, des courses et des jeux, the Tribunal administratif du Québec, Commission des norms du travail, Régie du Logement to name a few) in order to strongly safeguard and defend the interests of their clients.

Insurance Law

Our team ensures that the interests of our clients are efficiently represented, ranging from negotiations with various insurance providers to providing legal representation before the Courts.

Whether the case pertains to damage insurance, sickness and invalidity, or life insurance, as well as professional liability, our attorneys offer an adapted professional service of exceptional quality to each of their clients.

Peter Karavoulias

Founder - Attorney

Me Karavoulias works primarily in corporate and commercial law. His field of expertise includes the areas of bankruptcy and insolvency, real estate and business law, as well as civil litigation in general. Skilled negotiator and litigator, Me Karavoulias offers his solid experience and expertise at your disposal to obtain a fully customized legal solution to all your needs.

View Bio

Tessa Roy-Hébert

Attorney

Tessa Roy-Hébert joined our team as a lawyer in June 2017, after a career in civil and commercial litigation. In 2014, Ms. Roy-Hébert completed her Bachelor of Law degree at the Université du Québec à Montréal. During her studies, she spent one term at the Faculty of Law at the University of Nice - Sophia Antipolis in France. Ms. Roy-Hébert completed her Juris Doctor at the Université de Montréal in 2017. She is currently completing her master's degree in North American common law at the Université de Montréal. She wrote her dissertation on multi-jurisdictional collective actions. Her practical experience in civil and commercial litigation as well as in labor law and succession law has allowed her to develop a strong interest in advocacy and client relations.
